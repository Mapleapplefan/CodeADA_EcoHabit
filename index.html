EcoHabit

Curious about how your carbon footprint affects the future? Lets go back in time to the 1950's of Hill Valley to find out!

Rules for the game:

  • Use arrow keys to move!
  • This game is a choose your adventure! Use your cursor to select your answers. Be honest!
  • Look around the town of Hill Valley! Recognize any references?
  • At the very end of the game you will be given a personalized carbon footprint calculation.
